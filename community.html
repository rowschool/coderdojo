Sponsors

Become a sponsor!

We're looking for long-term partners who are invested in K-12 STEAM education in the Denver metropolitan area. CoderDojo Centennial is accepting donations in the form of:

  • computer equipment (extension cords, cables, laptops, monitors, USB keyboards and mice) and bins to carry it in
  • t-shirts for mentors and junior mentors
  • food for breakfast, snacks and drinks our sessions
  • financial contributions for website domain registration and to send members of our team to regional and global CoderDojo conventions
  • scholarships for CoderDojo students

Please reach out to the CoderDojo Centennial mentorship team if you are interested in contributing toward helping advance coding knowledge!
