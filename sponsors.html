Sponsors

Become a sponsor!

Would you like to sponsor CoderDojo Pittsburgh? We thank our sponsors immensely at the beginning and ending of every CoderDojo Pittsburgh session, and feature their names on the website.

We're looking for long-term partners who are invested in K-12 STEAM education in the Pittsburgh area. CoderDojo Pittsburgh is accepting donations in the form of:

  • computer equipment (extension cords, cables, laptops, monitors, USB keyboards and mice) and bins to carry it in
  • t-shirts for mentors and junior mentors
  • food for breakfast, snacks and drinks our sessions
  • financial contributions for website domain registration and to send members of our team to regional and global CoderDojo conventions
  • scholarships for CoderDojo students

Please reach out to the CoderDojo Pittsburgh mentorship team if you are interested in contributing toward helping advance coding knowledge!
