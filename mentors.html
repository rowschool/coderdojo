Become a junior mentor!

Junior mentors are students ages 7-17 who want to gain mentorship and leadership skills in addition to working on projects like other students who attend CoderDojo.

Junior mentors are the go-to for assisting other ninjas and their parents/guardians - greeting and welcoming newcomers, directing them to tables and helping them get on WiFi to get started on session work. Junior mentors also get a CoderDojo mentor t-shirt to signify their achievement (pending!).

Ninjas can become junior mentors if they:

  • have attended at least 5 CoderDojo sessions
  • have achieved level 1 mastery in any language
  • can arrive at 8:15am to help set up for the day
